E Minor Chord

The E minor chord is one of the most basic guitar chords, it has a melancholic/sad sound. It's a great chord; it fits many genres of music, such as pop, country, blues, and hip-hop.
To the right, you can see a diagram for this chord.
Start by placing your index finger on the 2nd fret of the A string, and then place your middle finger on the 2nd fret of the D string.
Before you start strumming the chord, make sure that your fingers are right above the fret, not in the middle, not over the fret, right above.
This is very important because if your fingers are not in the right position, the chord will buzz, trust me, I know from unfortunate experience.
Things to remember:
  • Make sure your fingers are right above the fret.
  • Make sure you press the string with enough force.
  • Make sure to strum all the strings.
  • Ensure your wrist is comfortable - pain indicates improper technique.
  • Practice makes perfect.
