C Major Chord

C major chord is a fundamental chord with a cheerful, uplifting sound. It is suitable for various music genres, such as pop, rock, and hip-hop.
To the right, you can see a diagram for this chord.
Basics: Start by placing your index finger on the 1st fret of the B string, Then place your middle finger on the 2nd fret of the D string, lastly Place your ring finger on the 3rd fret of the A string.
Before you start strumming the chord, make sure that your fingers are right above the fret, not in the middle, not over the fret, right above.
This is very important because if your fingers are not in the right position, the chord will buzz, trust me, I know from unfortunate experience.

Strings that you should strum are A, D, G, B, and high e string.
E A D G B e
✕ ✓ ✓ ✓ ✓ ✓
Be patient; it may take time to get used to the finger positions.
This chord can be tricky for beginners, but with practice, you will play it smoothly.
