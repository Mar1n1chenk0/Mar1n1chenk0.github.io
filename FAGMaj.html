G Major Chord

The G major chord is one of the most basic, but Simultaneously important chords for guitar.
The chord has an uplifting/happy sound, is great for many genres of music.
To the right, you can see a diagram for this chord.
Start by placing your middle finger on the 3rd fret of the low E string(the thickest string), then place your index finger on the 2nd fret of the A string, and finally place your ring finger on the 3rd fret of the high E string(thinnest string).
Before you start strumming the chord, make sure that your fingers are right above the fret, not in the middle, not over the fret, right above.
This is very important because if your fingers are not in the right position, the chord will buzz, trust me, I know from unfortunate experience.

The key of G major:

1 2 3 4 5 6 7
G A B C D E F#
Major Minor Major Minor Dimin.
