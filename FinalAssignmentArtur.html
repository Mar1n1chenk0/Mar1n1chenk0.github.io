This website serves as a guide to the acoustic guitar, specifically focusing on the G, Em, C, and D chords. These chords are in the key of G and are essential while being relatively easy for a beginner to learn. Below are a few songs that you can play simply by knowing how to play these four chords.

Some famous songs that use these chords:
  • Good Riddance - Green Day
    • These chords are used throughout the entire song.
  • Someone Like You - Adele
    • Adele used G, Em, C, and D chords in her song.
  • Let It Be - The Beatles
    • This world-renowned band has used just these four simple chords in their song.
  • Country Roads - John Denver
    • These chords are used for almost the entire song, with an F chord appearing at the end.
      However, the F chord is so brief that 97% of the song features the four chords mentioned above.

Note that these are simplified versions of the songs for easy strumming and singing.
For more authentic versions, visit Ultimate Guitar (Advanced Tabs & Chords)
However, be aware that the versions on that site may be significantly harder for a beginner.
